\subsection{Cross-Chain Liquidity Concentration}

The Teleport bridge imports native liquidity from Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and TON into the D-Chain execution environment. D-Chain's 1ms CLOB matching, concentrated AMM pools, and perpetual futures contracts concentrate execution into a single venue, eliminating cross-venue fragmentation. Yield-bearing wrappers (yLBTC, yLETH) allow LP capital to earn staking yield while providing liquidity, improving capital efficiency relative to idle bridge-wrapped tokens.
