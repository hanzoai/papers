\section{Hanzo Platform Overview}

\label{sec:platform}
% ============================================================================

The Hanzo Platform is a PaaS for AI-native application deployment. It treats LLM
inference, vector databases, model serving, and GPU compute as first-class
primitives~\cite{hanzoplatform}. The platform consists of the following services:

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\small
\begin{tabular}{llp{5.5cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Service} & \textbf{Namespace} & \textbf{Function} \\
\midrule
IAM & hanzo.id & OIDC identity, org scoping, JWT \\
Gateway & api.hanzo.ai & API routing, rate limiting, auth injection \\
KMS & kms.hanzo.ai & Secrets management (Infisical) \\
Commerce & commerce.hanzo.ai & Payment, subscriptions, invoicing \\
Insights & insights.hanzo.ai & Analytics, telemetry, conversion tracking \\
Base & base.hanzo.ai & Application database (PostgreSQL + FerretDB) \\
MCP & mcp.hanzo.ai & Model Context Protocol tool registry \\
Agent SDK & agents.hanzo.ai & Multi-agent orchestration framework \\
LLM Gateway & llm.hanzo.ai & Unified model serving (26 Zen models) \\
Platform & platform.hanzo.ai & Deployment, monitoring, CI/CD \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\caption{Hanzo Platform services. All services authenticate via Hanzo IAM and scope data to the requesting organization.}
\label{tab:services}
\end{table}

\subsection{Production Scale}

As of March 2026, the Hanzo Platform serves:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.1em]
    \item 2,800+ deployed applications across 1,200+ organizations
    \item 47M+ inference requests processed (cumulative)
    \item 26 Zen models (coder, artist, scribe, designer, analyst, etc.)
    \item 10 published defense papers (agentic AI, formal verification, quantum sensing)
    \item 99.7\% uptime SLA on LLM Gateway
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Integration with Lux Network}

Hanzo Network runs as a subnet of the Lux Network, leveraging Lux's
multi-consensus architecture for settlement finality. The Lux C-Chain (chain ID
96369) hosts AMM contracts (V2/V3) and DEX precompiles that enable HANZO trading
on the native Lux Exchange~\cite{luxwhitepaper}. Cross-chain bridging between
Hanzo Network and Lux C-Chain uses the Teleport protocol (LP-6010).
